About Me

My Story

I am a 30-year-old professional, born and raised in the captivating landscapes of southwest Missouri. With an unwavering passion for art and a keen interest in the realms of fantasy and sci-fi, I have spent a significant part of my life exploring the endless possibilities of creativity. As a digital artist, I find immense joy in bringing my imagination to life on a virtual canvas.
While I have always appreciated the visual arts, my love for board games, particularly Dungeons & Dragons, has further fueled my creative spirit. Delving into rich narratives and immersive worlds has not only provided me with countless hours of enjoyment, but it has also expanded my ability to think critically, problem-solve, and collaborate with others.
Having spent the past 8 years in the ecommerce industry, I recently made the decision to embark on a career change, transitioning into the field of front-end development. This shift was driven by my inherent desire to create, coupled with a fascination for finding innovative solutions through technology. The prospect of utilizing my creativity in conjunction with technical skills to build captivating user experiences captivated me and inspired me to explore the front-end development industry.
I thrive on the challenge of crafting interactive and visually appealing websites and applications. My diverse background in art, coupled with my experience in ecommerce, has equipped me with a unique perspective in creating seamless user interfaces. I am continuously honing my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other front-end technologies to ensure that I can effectively bring designs to life and provide exceptional user experiences.
Driven by an unwavering passion for creating things in unique and imaginative ways, I am eager to contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of front-end development. I am committed to staying at the forefront of industry trends and embracing new technologies that enhance the digital experience. Collaborating with multidisciplinary teams and engaging in continuous learning are core principles that guide my professional journey.
If you are seeking a front-end developer who combines technical proficiency with a deep-rooted passion for creativity, I am confident that my skills and enthusiasm make me a valuable asset to any project. I am excited to bring my expertise, dedication, and innovative mindset to contribute to the development of captivating digital experiences.
